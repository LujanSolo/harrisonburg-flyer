Visit Harrisonburg

From the heights of Skyline Drive to the halls of James Madison University, this small Virginia city is rich with history.

Great things to do around Harrisonburg

Attend the Theatre

James Madison University has a magnificent theatre program with shows of all types.

Hike Skyline Drive

The views here will take your breath away as you take in the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Tour Historic Downtown

Nightlife, good food, and the historic courthouse (built in 1940) show off a rich tapestry of history.
